Hackathons

Our hackathons provide an exciting platform for members to collaborate, compete, and develop creative solutions to real-world challenges.

Why Participate

  • Build Real Projects - Turn your ideas into working prototypes in a fast-paced environment.
  • Team Collaboration - Work with talented developers, designers, and problem-solvers.
  • Learn Fast - Hackathons are the best way to learn new technologies under pressure.
  • Win Prizes - Top teams receive recognition, prizes, and opportunities to showcase their work.

How It Works

Teams of 3-5 participants are formed at the start of the event. You will have 24-48 hours to build a solution based on a theme or challenge. Mentors are available throughout to provide guidance and support. At the end, teams present their projects to a panel of judges.

Who Can Join

Anyone with a passion for building things! You don't need to be an expert programmer. Designers, project managers, and idea people are all welcome. We provide the resources, you bring the creativity.
