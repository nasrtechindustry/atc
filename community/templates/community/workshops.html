Workshops

Our workshops provide hands-on learning experiences led by industry experts and experienced community members. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced developer, there is always something new to learn.

What We Cover

  • Python Programming - From fundamentals to advanced concepts like async programming and decorators.
  • Artificial Intelligence - Introduction to machine learning, deep learning, and building AI-powered applications.
  • Web Development - Build modern web applications using Django, Flask, and frontend technologies.
  • Networking - Understand computer networks, protocols, and cyber security fundamentals.

Workshop Format

Each workshop is structured to include both theory and practice. You will work on real-world projects, collaborate with peers, and receive guidance from mentors. Workshops typically run for 2-4 hours and are held on weekends.

Who Can Attend

Anyone with an interest in technology! Our workshops are designed to accommodate different skill levels. Beginners are especially welcome, and we provide all the resources you need to get started.
